Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Question: Who can sign up for SimpleBook services?

The SimpleBook online services is open to all Singapore Citizens anad Permanent Residents with a valid email address.

Question: I forgot my password. How can I recover it to gain back my access?

Please click on the 'Forget Password' link on the Login Page to reset your password through your registered email address.

Question: Who are the people behind this website?

We are a group of final year students completing our studies at the University of Wollongong, and we hope that through SimpleBook, users can achieve ultimate convenience by avoiding long waits at health institutions and also live a healthier lifestyle through our health promotion messages.

Question: What are some of the additional features I have access to when I sign up?

Besides being able to book medical appointments online, users will also be able to receive reminders on their upcoming appointments, and as well as being able to receive health reminder messages.

Question: Is signing up for SimpleBook free?

Signing for up SimpleBook and using our services is absolutely free and requires no additional costs.
